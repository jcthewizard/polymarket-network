Understanding the Visualization

Node size = Trading volume
Link thickness = Correlation strength (0.5 - 1.0)

Data & Correlation

Markets are filtered to show those with >$50k volume and 5-95% probability. Data refreshes automatically every 10 minutes from Polymarket.

How Correlations Work:

We use log-return correlation ‚Äî this measures whether markets move up and down on the same days, not just whether they trend together.

Only pairs with >50% correlation are shown as connected.
